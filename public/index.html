Rubiks Demo

Use the mouse to rotate the cube model around in space. You can rotate the faces clockwise by using the "q", "w", and "e" keys, where "a", "s", and "d" rotate the respective opposite facing sides. Holding the spacebar and then pressing either of those buttons will cause the same side to rotate, but in the counter-clockwise direction instead. More functionality to come soon...
